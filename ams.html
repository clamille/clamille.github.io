Aim

This session has the special aim of bringing together those working on the algebraic and analytic aspects of singularities, especially with regard to homological aspects of singularity theory, such as those related to differential forms, indices of holomorphic vector fields, Hochschild (co)homology and invariants related to the classic Milnor and Tjurina numbers.
